Costiq is an independent product comparison platform built to help users evaluate everyday products sold online using clear, factual, and transparent criteria. We do not sell products. We help you decide.

Our comparison principles

Independence

Products are selected based on relevance and availability, not brand relationships.

Transparency

We clearly explain why products are included and who they are suitable for.

Practical focus

Comparisons are designed for everyday buying decisions, not marketing claims.

Who Costiq is for

  • Users comparing similar products before purchase
  • Users looking for practical, everyday options

Who it is not for

  • Users seeking premium or professional-grade solutions
  • Users requiring specialized or niche performance
